My first experience in the field of
Computer Science



I am Dilpreet Singh, from Amritsar. I completed my +2 from Guru Teg Bahadur Public School, Khankot.Then I got admission in G.N.D.U. Amritsar for B.Tech in the field of Computer Science. I was very much happy and excited. From my chidhood I had great interest in computers and related things.

When I started coming to class I found that there was no subject related to computers in my first Semester. I was quite annoyed. Then in the Orientation Program by our H.O.D. sir, I came to know about the CESS team in our department. Then after few days, we had first interaction wih CESS team when they call us at M.R.S.Audi to give us information about various teams in CESS.

After few days, they started conducting classes for coding in HTML and CSS. These classes were very much useful and informative.

I am very thankful to them for all that they did for us. I really enjoyed these classes and understood what HTML $ CSS is and how to code in these.
